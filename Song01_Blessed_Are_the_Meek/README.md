# Song 1 Blessed Are the Meek - சாந்தகுணமுள்ளவனே பூமியைச் சுதந்தரி

This song of joyful inheritance echoes Psalm 37 — the meek shall inherit the land and delight in abundant peace; trust in the Lord, commit your way, wait patiently, and He will act. It calls the remnant to humility, trust, and upright living — the wicked fade, but the righteous inherit forever. In the eternal day, this promise is fulfilled — we inherit the new earth with the Lamb (Revelation 21:7).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, who gives the kingdom to the meek,
thank You for abundant peace and perfect inheritance.
Let us trust, wait, walk uprightly.
In the eternal day, let us inherit the earth with You forever.
Come quickly, our Peace and King.
