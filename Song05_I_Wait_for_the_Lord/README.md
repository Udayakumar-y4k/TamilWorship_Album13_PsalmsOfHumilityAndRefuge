# I Wait for the Lord - கர்த்தருக்காக பொறுமையாக என்றென்றும் காத்திருப்பேன்

This song of patient waiting and joyful deliverance echoes Psalm 40 — “I waited patiently for the Lord; He inclined to me and heard my cry. He drew me up from the pit of destruction, out of the miry bog, and set my feet upon a rock.” It is a testimony of God’s rescue, new song, and call to trust. In the eternal day, this waiting is fulfilled — no more pit, only the solid rock of the Lamb forever (Revelation 22:1–2).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, who hears the patient cry and sets feet on the rock,
thank You for inclining to us and giving a new song.
Let us wait on You with hope, sing Your praise in the assembly.
In the eternal day, let us stand firm on Your rock forever.
Come quickly, our Deliverer and Song.
